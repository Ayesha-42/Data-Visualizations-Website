Team techCreate came about when 2 Swinburne University Computer Science students formed a team to work together on a Major Project on Sustainability and Waste Management, and its impacts on the World. The team crossed the Continental divide, with Ayesha being based in India, and Dean based in Australia. The team members communicated via the Discord instant messaging application, and versioning software used was Google Drive.
